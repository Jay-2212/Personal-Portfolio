# Financial Disclaimer

This content ships verbatim (or near-verbatim, allowing for minor export-format
adaptation) at the bottom of the Word proposal, inside the Excel workbook's
Assumptions sheet, and on the results dashboard footer. Don't paraphrase it per
surface — consistency here matters more than tone-matching each surface.

---

**This tool is a decision-support calculator, not financial, investment, tax, or
legal advice.**

Every figure in this report — revenue, cost, cash flow, ROI, NPV, IRR, payback period,
and the Investment Outlook score — is calculated from the assumptions you entered,
combined in some cases with directional industry benchmarks. It does not represent a
guarantee, forecast, or professional appraisal of any equipment purchase.

**On the assumptions:**

- Fields you entered directly (purchase cost, your vendor's quoted AMC/CMC rate, your
  actual loan terms, your hospital's own utilization data, and similar) reflect what
  you told the tool, not independently verified figures.
- Fields left at a suggested default are labeled with a confidence level (High /
  Medium / Low) and a source, or explicitly marked as having no available benchmark.
  A benchmark, even a High-confidence one, describes a general pattern across
  hospitals or a regulatory norm — it is not a substitute for your own vendor
  quotation, tariff sheet, payer contract, or lender sanction letter. See each field's
  tooltip for its specific source and confidence, and `content/benchmark-notes.md` for
  how to read that.
- Some benchmark ranges are based on limited data (a single tender, a single
  hospital's reported cost, or a government reimbursement ceiling rather than a
  private cash tariff) and may not reflect your hospital's actual scale, city, payer
  mix, or negotiating position.

**Before acting on this report:**

Replace every benchmark-sourced figure with your own vendor quotation, tariff sheet,
payer contracts, and lender's actual sanction terms wherever they're available. Have
the resulting model reviewed by your organization's finance team, chartered
accountant, or financial advisor before using it to support an actual capital
expenditure decision, loan application, or board presentation.

**No guarantee of outcome:** actual utilization, realization, collection timelines,
maintenance costs, and financing terms will differ from any assumption entered here.
Past benchmark data and typical industry ranges are not predictive of future
performance for any specific hospital or piece of equipment.
